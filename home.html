Swinburne/CalTech Transients Workshop


5 - 7 October 2015                                        Workshop

Old text:
A two and a half day workshop to highlight the latest science from the Keck telescopes. This inaugural workshop will bring the 'Keck family' to Swinburne University in Australia to share their common interests and scientific results from the world's largest optical telescopes.

There is no registration fee to attend the Workshop. However, please let us know of your attendance by filling out our registration page. Although the registration deadline has passed, late registration is still open and requests for talks will be placed on a reserve list. Talks requested by participants coming to the Workshop from outside of Australia will be given priority.

The Workshop will take place in Swinburne Place South, room SPS 136 on the Swinburne University Hawthorn campus. Click here for a campus map.
